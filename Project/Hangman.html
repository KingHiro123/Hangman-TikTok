The Hangman Game

TikTok Youth Camp 2022

We are a group of students from various Polytechnics, coming together to create our very own webpage!


How to play?

- A random word and a category will be generated

- Arrays of letters will be given

- Each incorrect letter chosen will be reflected on the screen

- Keep guessing till you get the word!

- Once the stickman is formed, you lose the round.
